Street Fighter

Street Fighter is a fighting game released in 1987. Since the release of the original there have been a total of 5 games realeased in the main line and numerous spinoffs. Street Fighter II surpasses the populatiry of the original game tremendously and added more features that would become common in fighting games to follow. In 1996, Capcom teamed up with Marvel to release X-Men vs Street Fighter. This would be followed in the coming years with the Marvel vs Capcom series of video games. The Street Fighter games have continued up to Street Fighter V currently. There have also been several movies in this series.

Fun Facts!

  • A special version of the Street Fighter arcade consoles was produced which featured pressure sensitive pads instead of traditional arcade buttons for controls.

  • Several Street Fighter characters didn't gain first or last names until the release of the movies.

  • There have been at least 8 versions of Street Fighter II released to-date. Street Fighter II is estimated to have grossed about $10 million in revenue.
